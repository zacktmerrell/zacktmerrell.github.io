Full Body Bored Ape Mock-up

Digital Illustration, 2021

This was a fun project I created while all of the NFT craze and crypto currency was a much bigger conversation. I wanted to create this project, not only due to the fact that they were in constant conversation, but also because I liked some of the designs but wanted to see what I could do.

I think that the full body look with the toga really captures the stereotypical college look that I was going for; the red cup and the folder in the hands are great hints of that. I was in college at the time of creating this, making it a very time appropriate piece.
