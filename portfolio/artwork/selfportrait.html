Low Poly Self Portrait

Digital Illustration, 2017

Another low poly portrait. This is actually the first and only self portrait that I have ever done. Eyes have always been a struggle for me in digital portraiture, so I decided to take a picture with glasses on in order to side step that issue. I think for a first self portrait, and an early low poly piece from me in 2017, it came out quiet nice. It is a much more choatic or free version of the low poly artwork that I have created recently; with some triangles free floating in the middle of others as well as even the rare non-triangle polygon.
