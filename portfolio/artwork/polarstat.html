Polar Bear Statistics Project

Statistic Design, 2018

This was a project in which I had to use some kind of statistic in my project. I used the graph of the decrease in ice habitat for polar bears as the structure for the mountains in the back. To further the piece I drew a polar bear looking sad on a melting slab of ice, just kind of floating in the foreground of the image.
