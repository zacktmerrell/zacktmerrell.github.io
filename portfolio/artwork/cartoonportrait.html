Cartoon Portrait

Digital Illustration, 2021

This was a piece that I was contacted to create for a friends graduation. Flyers for a party were to be created and an image of the graduate was requested for the front of those flyers.

I wanted to try a type of art that I am not the most comfortable with. I created a cartoon style piece by blobing big sections of color and layering them all together.
