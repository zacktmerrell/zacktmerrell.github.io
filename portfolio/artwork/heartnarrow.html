Heart 'n' Arrow

Digital Illustration, 2021

This piece, Heart 'n' Arrow, is one of the most recent low poly pieces I have created. The piece is actually part of a bigger collection of digital illustrations that has been in progress since the start of the year. As a way of keeping myself creating art on a consistent basis, I started an instagram page for my art, (which you kind find by clicking the instagram icon below) that I have been posting on a weekly basis at minimum; this was one of those projects that has made it to the instagram page.
