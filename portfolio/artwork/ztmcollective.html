ZTM Collective Logo

Logo Design, 2021

This logo was made for the Instagram account that I started at the beginning of 2021. I liked doing this because I was able to design exactly what I wanted, instead of having to check in with a customer going back and forth about what they want. On top of that, this was for my own art Instagram so I was able to make it authentically me and didn't have to design it to fit some theme.


The second picture is the updated version of my logo (2022), due to the fact I change my tag from ZTM Illustrations to ZTM Collective. This change is due to the fact that the word collective better describes my work ability much better than illustrations.
