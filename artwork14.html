Nu Alpha Kappa, Alpha Chapter Flag

Flag Design, 2020

This is the current Alpha Chapter Flag for Nu Alpha Kappa. I created this flag shortly after I crossed, because it had been a few years since it had been updated. This project was very simple due to the fact that I already had information on what it should look like and the colors needed. The hardest part was masking out the temple and Chicano man, two symbols used frequently in this fraternity.
