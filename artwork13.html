Polar Bear Statistics Project

Flyer Design, 2018

This was a flyer I made for a class assignment where we were tasked to create a flyer about a current event or issue. I have always been a huge believer in global warming and the fact that we are slowly destroying this planet, so I decided to run with that topic. I wanted to capture the idea that planet we live on is very fragile and that we need to take better care of it, so I decided to create this image of the world being an egg. I pasted the continents onto the yolk of the egg and egg whites spilling away from the yolk relate to the melting of ice caps in the North and South pole. Finally, I added a little bit of type to slam the point home, and tied it in further to the image by making the type the same color as the egg yolk.
