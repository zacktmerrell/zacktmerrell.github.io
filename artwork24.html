Peace Tea Wall


Found Item Sculpture, 2017-Present

About 3 years has come to this. Over 225 cans have been bought, consumed, and stuck to the wall behind my desk to change the entire look of my workspace. I am so happy that this thing has finally been finished!

Besides the fact that the cans are make up a beautiful wall of art, I also think the the symbol on these cans is something that is extremely current. I am not someone to rant about politics, but I just want to put the message out there to spread peace, love, and positivity. You never know what someone is going through, you have no clue of what someone is holding in from everyone else.
BE KIND
SPREAD PEACE, LOVE, AND POSITIVITY
