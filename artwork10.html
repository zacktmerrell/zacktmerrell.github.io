Nu Alpha Kappa Rush Flyer

Flyer Design, 2021

This flyer was made for my fraternities instagram account, as an announcement of our rush calendar for the semester. I had to work with our organizations rush advisor to make sure it had the information he needed, as well as look the way he wanted. After getting that first step of approval, I had to go to the fraternities national board in order to get their approval for the chapter to post the flyer on instagram. Due to conflicts with previous designs and information, I had to update and change the flyer multiple times as shown.
