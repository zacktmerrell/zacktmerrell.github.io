N.F.B.M. (2018)

This is a collection of photos that I did in memory of my grandpa who passed away in the late months of 2016. These photos were taken around my hometown of Santa Cruz, California, and they are all photos that remind me of my grandpa in some way. The first photo in this series sticks out the most because not only is it of a sticker containing the title of the collection itself, but actually because the car the sticker is on was once my grandpas and is now mine.
