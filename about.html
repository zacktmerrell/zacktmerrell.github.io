My practice illustrates geometric designs with some ethics and inspirations grabbed from skateboarding and inspiration from nature. With influences as diverse as Jim Phillips, this form of new media art is crafted from both traditional geometric abstract values and the modern dialogues of graffiti and skateboard art. The simplicity of geometric art combined with the modern graffiti colors makes the work really jump off the screen or any other medium where the art happens to be displayed.
