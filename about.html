I'm Zack Merrell, a new media artist from the Bay Area, focused on digital illustration and design.



With influences as local as Jim Phillips and skateboard culture, this form of new media art is crafted from both traditional geometric abstract values and the modern dialogues of graffiti and skateboard art. The simplicity of geometric art, combined with the modern colors of street art and graffiti, makes the work really jump off the screen or any other medium where the art happens to be displayed.

Our connection to nature is not only beneficial for us humans, but as well for nature itself. Through my digital illustrations, I strive to bring the purity of nature into my computer and create a more geometrical form. Changing the smooth organic lines of nature and creating a more sharp version brings the organic into the digital world.

In my personal designs, I strive to create materials for current events to educate and inspire. For my design work contracted by a client, I look to bring their wants to life through a variety of digital programs, working back and forth with them, and ultimately reaching an end product.

Outside of my artwork, I play soccer for the SJSU Mens Club Soccer team and enjoy music, the ocean, and driving.
