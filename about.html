I'm Zack Merrell, a new media artist and digital designer located in the Silicon Valley



I strive to use my skills to design and bring to life what was just a thought or idea, as well as create pieces that tell stories about emotion, mental health and many more.

I have been immersed in digital art for nearly a decade now. Currently, I'm working as a Freelancer-designing anything from logos and business cards to social media posts, and creating visually beautiful illustrations.

Outside of my artwork, I enjoy playing soccer, music, nature, and driving.
