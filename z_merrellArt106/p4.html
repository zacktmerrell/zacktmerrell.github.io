The Big Four Circuits in the Bread Board Test

The video is the video that I took from the In-class bread board test. This video, along with the test, showed the big four circuits that we discussed in lecture. This includes a digital input, an analog input, a digital output to a LED, and a digital output to a speaker.

Final Micro Box

These are pictures of my finished Micro Box. As you can see, it includes 2 LEDs, a speaker, 2 analog potentiometer, a momentary button, and a toggle button. The toggle button acts as the on/off switch for the entire box, while the momentary button will cause the speaker to play a different song if held down. The video at the very bottom shows the Micro Box in action as in plays the two MIDI songs that I inputted, Crazy Train and Another One Bites the Dust.

Due to me doing this documentation later on in the semester due to problems with my Cyberduck and the VPN, I have the photocells and neopixels on the box from the Serial UI assignment that came after. The Micro Box for this assignment is purely the middle section.
