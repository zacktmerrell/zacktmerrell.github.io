Machine Companion

For this assignment we had to make a machine companion for ourselves that could be there with us every day to help us or make the day easier in some way.

Below is the image of my companion that I made. For for my companion you can see that I created something small, almost similar to a parrot on your shoulder, but the eye would hover right next to your head when traveling with your body. However the great thing about this companion is that you can send it out for you to either record something for you, for example a lecture, or you can speak and see through the companion in real time as well. This makes communicating with your peers while you can not be physically present much easier and as close to normal as possible.
