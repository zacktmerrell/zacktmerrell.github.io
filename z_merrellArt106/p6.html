Brush_Bot

For this assignment we had to expand on our Micro Box by adding in 2 photocells and 3 new neopixel leds. These LEDs are RGB and can do pretty much any color on the color wheel making them fun to play with.

With the Micro Box, we had to run a program that communicated from Processing to Arduino and then do one that communicated Arduino to Processing. Below are pictures of the final box, with videos showing what the box does with the software.
