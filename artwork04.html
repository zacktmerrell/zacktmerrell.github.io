Low Poly Self Portrait

Digital Illustration, 2017

Another low poly portrait. This is actually the first and only self portrait that I have ever done. Eyes have always been a struggle for me so I decided to take a picture with glasses on in order to side step that issue. I think for a first self portrait, and an early low poly piece from me in 2017, it came out quiet nice.
