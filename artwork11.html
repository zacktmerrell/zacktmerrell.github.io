Nu Alpha Kappa Valentine's Day Raffle

Flyer Design, 2021

As the current chapter fundraising chair for Nu Alpha Kappa at San Jose State University, I am able combine the chapters fundraising ideas with my ability to create logos, flyers, and more. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, fundraising has been a little more difficult, however, with the use of social media and the internet we have been able to hold a few online raffles; this one for Valentine's Day. I decided to display this flyer because I went outside of my comfort zone and used parts of Adobe Illustrator I do not normally use in order to create the pink cloud effect in the background. The final product does not have our fraternities letters on it because we were not able to get approval from the school to hold a raffle that was ran by the chapter itself.
