Stamp | Fusion Creation

The first section of this assignment was the creation of the stamp I used at the specific locations later on in this project.

These pictures show the creation of the stamp in Fusion 360. For my stamp I did a a symbol that is my intials, Z-T-M, all on top of each other. I wanted to use this because it looks pretty abstract when in this form, but in the literal sense it symbolizes me in the most basic way possible.
