Money is the byproduct of doing what you love
Part 1

For part one, my group decided to go with the word Accretion. The definition of the word is as stated; the process of growth or increase, typically by the gradual accumulation of additional layers or matter.

So for my piece I decided to take a bunch of photos that I had taken over the summer of me and my family wake surfing, and layer them all together with 10% opacity. I wanted to do this because I take a lot of photos but most of them end up just sitting on an SD card or my computer, while only a select few really ever get passed around. I thought this would be a perfect opportunity to actually use these photos for a project and actually give them some use.

Part 2

For part 2, we just simply showed two pieces of work that we had made so far in our DMA journey. The two pieces I decided to pick were pieces that I felt showcased where I started and what I am doing now. The low poly piece was done back in 2016 and that piece reminds me of the start to this process through art. The logo is something that I did much more recently and more of the professional side to the art I have been doing. For the logo I actaully got to work with a client and go back and fourth until they got exactly what they wanted.
