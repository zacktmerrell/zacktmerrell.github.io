Invasion Car Club Logo

Logo Design, 2021

For this logo I was contacted to recreate the logo Invasion Car Club currently had. The customer wanted the same from the original, but have the San Francisco skyline in the background instead of the truck and bridge. Finally, the customer asked that the new logo say C.C.(Car Club) instead of the current T.C.(Truck Club).

Due to not having the original logo file or information about the fonts and artwork used in the original logo, I was tasked to recreate the typeface used in the orginal logo. Since the name of the car club itself was not chaning, it made things a bit easier, as I onloy had to spend the time tracing and recreating letters that already existed. The skyline and bridge in the background were super easy to create using the pen tool in Adobe Illustrator. I experimented with a few different placements of the words "Bay Area" but the customer liked the placement on the strings of the bridge.
